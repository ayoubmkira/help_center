Getting Started with Devaito

6 min read

Welcome to Devaito! Whether you're a business owner, a creative professional, or someone looking to establish an online presence, you've come to the right place. Devaito offers a user-friendly website builder that enables you to bring your ideas to life.

Our platform goes beyond just helping you create a stunning website. You can also manage your entire business directly from your Devaito account — from contacts and products to services and sales. Enhance your business with tools for email marketing, social media posts, and site analytics to track your progress.

Step 1 | Create a Free Account

Signing up with Devaito is completely free. You can create a fully functional website and manage your business without any costs. Plus, you can build as many websites as you like under one account. Learn more about building a website for free.

Once your site is ready and your business set up, you can choose to upgrade to a Premium plan, which unlocks features like custom domain connections, removal of ads, and much more.

Sign up to create a free account with Devaito.

Step 2 | Build Your Site

Devaito provides 800+ website templates to help you start building your site. These templates have been carefully designed to meet the latest trends and industry standards.

All templates are fully customizable. You can alter the layout, design, and content to suit your preferences. Add apps, change styles, and create a site that perfectly fits your brand and business needs.

Customize everything using our intuitive drag-and-drop editor, and add elements like image galleries, video backgrounds, animations, and more to make your site stand out.

If you're looking for a completely unique design, you can also start with a blank canvas and build your site from scratch.

Step 3 | Set Up Your Business

Devaito gives you the flexibility to build a website tailored to your needs. With over 250 apps and services available, you can sell products online, offer services, take bookings, share your thoughts on a blog, and showcase your work with a stunning portfolio.

Our platform supports businesses of all sizes, from personal brands to large enterprises.

Explore Devaito's Business Solutions to find the right tools for your business.

Step 4 | Choose a Premium Plan

While Devaito offers a free plan, you might want to consider upgrading to access additional features, such as:

  • Connecting your custom domain
  • Removing platform ads
  • Accepting online payments
  • Expanding your storage and bandwidth
  • Gaining access to advanced analytics
  • Premium customer support
Compare Premium Plans and Features

Tip :

Don't worry about long-term commitments. You can change or cancel your plan at any time, or even assign it to a different site within your account. Learn more about upgrading your plan.

Step 5 | (Optional) Set Up Payments

Easily accept payments for products or services directly through your website. Devaito offers a variety of payment methods based on your location.

Connect to Devaito Payments to manage all your transactions directly from your dashboard. If Devaito Payments isn’t available in your region, you can integrate with over 70 other payment providers globally, including Credit Cards, PayPal, and Manual Payments.

Depending on your chosen payment method, you may also offer options like Apple Pay, cryptocurrency payments, BNPL (Buy Now, Pay Later), and even Point of Sale systems.

Explore Payment Options for Your Site

Step 6 | Manage and Grow Your Business

  • Complete SEO Tools: Optimize your site to rank higher on search engines and increase visibility.
  • AI-Driven Ads: Create dynamic ad campaigns on Facebook and Instagram, powered by Devaito's smart algorithm to continually optimize your ads for better results.
  • Marketing Integrations: Engage your audience through email campaigns, social media, and more.
  • Analytics Tools: Track your website's performance and audience behavior to make informed decisions that drive growth.

Stay connected and manage your business on the go with the Devaito app. Access everything you need to communicate with your team and monitor your site directly from your mobile device.

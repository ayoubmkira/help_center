Video background

2 min read

A video background can make a section more dynamic and engaging, adding movement and visual appeal to your website.

Set a Video Background

  1. Click on AI Sections Manager.
  2. Hover over the section you want to edit.
  3. Click the Section Background icon.
  4. Select the Video Background option.
  5. Choose a video or upload a new one.
  6. Adjust the following video background options:
    • Opacity: Adjust the transparency of the video background to blend it with the sectionâ€™s design.
    • Parallax: Enable this option to keep the background fixed while the section moves up or down.
    • Background Color: Add an overlay color to improve text readability or create a specific mood.
  7. Click on Apply button.

These options help you create a visually stunning background while maintaining readability and design balance.

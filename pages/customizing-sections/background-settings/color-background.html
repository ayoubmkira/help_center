Color background

2 min read

This option allows users to choose a solid color or no background at all for the section, providing flexibility in section design.

Set a Background Color

  1. Click AI Sections Manager.
  2. Hover over the section you want to edit.
  3. Click the Section Background icon.
  4. Select a background color option.
  5. Click AI Sections Manager.
  6. Click on Apply button.

These options give you control over whether to use a solid color background or leave the section without one.

Section image background

3 min read

Adding an image background enhances the section’s visual appeal and improves design consistency.

Set an image background

  1. Click on AI Sections Manager.
  2. Hover over the section you want to edit.
  3. Click the Section Background icon.
  4. Select the Image Background option.
  5. Choose an image or upload a new one.
  6. Adjust the following image background options:
    • Background Size: Adjusts how the image fits within the section (e.g., cover, contain, auto).
    • Background Repeat: Controls whether the image repeats across the section (e.g., no-repeat, repeat, repeat-x, repeat-y).
    • Background Position: Defines the placement of the image within the section (e.g., center, top, bottom, left, right).
    • Background Color: Adds a color overlay to enhance contrast or blend the image with the section.
    • Parallax: Enable this option to keep the background fixed while the section moves up or down.
    • Opacity: Adjusts the transparency level of the background image to create subtle or bold visual effects.
  7. Click on Apply button.

These options allow you to fully customize the section’s background to match your website’s design.

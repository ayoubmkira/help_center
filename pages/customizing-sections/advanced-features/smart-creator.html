Smart creator (AI)

2 min read

The Smart Creator allows you to quickly generate and customize sections using AI, making it easier to create engaging website layouts.

Use Smart Creator:

  1. Click AI Sections Manager.
  2. Hover over the section you want to edit.
  3. Click the Smart Creator button.
  4. Choose how you want to generate content:
    • Write Text: Enter a prompt to guide the AI in creating the section.
    • Regenerate Options: Click on buttons to regenerate specific elements, such as:
      • Regenerate Section: Create a completely new section.
      • Regenerate Text: Generate new text content.
      • Regenerate Images: Replace images with AI-generated ones.
  5. Click Generate to start the AI generating content.

With Smart Creator, you can efficiently design and modify sections while maintaining control over content and style.

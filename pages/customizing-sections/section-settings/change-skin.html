Change Skin

2 min read

The Skin option allows you to change the overall visual style of a section. You have three options for skin:

  • Light: Provides a lighter, more minimalistic design.
  • Dark: Offers a darker theme for a more dramatic and modern look.
  • Default: Keeps the default skin based on your theme settings.

Change skin

  • Click on AI Sections Manager.
  • Hover over the section you want to change.
  • Open the Section Settings panel.
  • Locate the Skin option.
  • Select the desired skin from the available options.
  • Click on Apply button.

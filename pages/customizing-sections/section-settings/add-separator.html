Add separator

2 min read

A separator helps create a visual division between sections, improving readability and design clarity.

Add Separator

  1. Click on AI Sections Manager.
  2. Hover over the section where you want to add a separator.
  3. Open the Section Settings panel.
  4. Locate the Separator option.
  5. Choose a separator type:
    • Content Width: Limits the separator to the section content's width.
    • Screen Width: Extends the separator across the entire screen.
  6. Click on Apply button.

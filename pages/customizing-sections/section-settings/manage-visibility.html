Manage visibility

2 min read

The Manage Visibility option allows you to control in which devices a section is displayed. You can choose to display the section on Mobile, Tablet, or Desktop views, or any combination of these.

Manage visibility

  1. Click on AI Sections Manager.
  2. Hover over the section you want to adjust.
  3. Open the Section Settings panel.
  4. Locate the Visibility option.
  5. Choose from the three available device options:
    • Mobile: Display the section on mobile devices.
    • Tablet: Display the section on tablet devices.
    • Desktop: Display the section on desktop devices.
  6. Click on Apply button.

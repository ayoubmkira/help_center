Adjust alignment

2 min read

The Manage Alignment option allows you to control the alignment of the content within a section.

You can choose to align the content to the Left, Right, or Center to adjust how it appears within the section.

Manage Alignment

  1. Click on AI Sections Manager.
  2. Hover over the section you want to adjust.
  3. Open the Section Settings panel.
  4. Locate the Align option.
  5. Choose from the three available alignment options:
    • Left: Aligns the content to the left side of the section.
    • Right: Aligns the content to the right side of the section.
    • Center: Aligns the content in the center of the section.
  6. Click on Apply button.

Signing Up for a Free Devaito Account

3 min read

One of the best things about Devaito is that signing up is completely free. You can build a fully functional website without spending a dime.

Once your website is live, you have the option to upgrade and unlock premium features such as connecting a custom domain, removing ads, and more.

Signing Up for a Devaito Account

You can sign up for Devaito using your email address, Google credentials, or a social media account.

To create your Devaito account:

  1. Visit the Devaito sign-up page.
  2. Choose your preferred registration method and enter your details.
  3. Click Sign Up and start building your website today!

For more details on upgrading your site, check out our premium plans.

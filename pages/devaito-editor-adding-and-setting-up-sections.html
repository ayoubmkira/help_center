Devaito Editor: Adding and Setting Up Sections

4 min read

Sections provide a simple way to structure and display content on your site.

They extend across the full width of your page, allowing you to customize spacing and elements. Sections also adapt to different screen sizes, ensuring an optimal experience for visitors on both desktop and mobile.

You can build your site by adding multiple sections, rearranging them, and customizing them to fit your needs.

Adding a Section to Your Site

Sections help organize your page content. You can choose from various pre-designed options with built-in layouts and elements.

To add a section:

  1. Open your editor.
  2. Click Sections manager on the left panel.
  3. Select a category.
  4. Drag you desired section and Drop it where you want.

Rearranging Sections on Your Page

Adjust the layout of your page by moving sections up or down to create a seamless experience for visitors.

To move a section:

  1. Open your editor.
  2. Click Sections manager on the left panel.
  3. Hover over the section you want to move.
  4. Use the arrows to adjust its position:
    • Move Down : Places the section below the one beneath it.
    • Move Up : Moves the section above the one on top.

Languages Available in Devaito

3 min read

With Devaito, you can build your website from almost anywhere in the world and in multiple languages.

You can also switch your Devaito account and editor to a different language and receive support from our Customer Care team in the language that suits you best.

Account and Editor Languages

Devaito and its editor are currently available in English, Spanish, Portuguese, French, German, Polish, Italian, Russian, Japanese, Korean, Turkish, Chinese, Thai, Swedish, Norwegian, Dutch, Danish, Czech, Bahasa Indonesia, Vietnamese, and Ukrainian.

The language of your dashboard and editor is based on your Devaito account settings. Learn how to update your account language.

Note :

Starting January 2024, Hindi will no longer be supported.

Changing Your Site’s Language

Your site's language influences everything, including the content displayed to visitors, as well as the emails and notifications they receive. It also affects app languages (e.g., Devaito Stores, Devaito Bookings, Devaito Chat) and browser-based translations when enabled.

Additionally, setting the correct language improves accessibility by helping assistive technologies adjust voice tone, pitch, and speed to match the chosen language.

You can update your site’s language from the Language & Region settings.

If you need multiple languages on your site, consider using Devaito Multilingual (see below).

Using Special Characters on Your Site

The Devaito editor supports all languages and special characters compatible with UTF-8, the dominant character encoding on the web. This includes Chinese, Japanese, Thai, Arabic, Korean, Greek, Russian, Portuguese, Hebrew, and more.

No additional setup is required—our editor automatically supports the languages available on your operating system. Simply ensure your computer has the necessary language installed, and start typing in any editable text field.

You can also add custom language fonts within the editor. Learn more about adding language-specific fonts.

Creating a Multilingual Site

If you want your site to be available in multiple languages, use **Devaito Multilingual**. This feature is ideal for businesses in tourist-heavy locations or websites with a global audience.

Devaito Multilingual supports over 180 languages. You can manually enter translations or use the auto-translate feature to simplify the process.

Learn more about Devaito Multilingual.

Edit icon

1 min read

Icons add visual appeal and clarity to your website. With the ability to easily change icons, you can enhance user interaction and design consistency across your site. Choose from a variety of icons to fit your style and purpose.

Edit an icon:

  1. Click on Text manager on the left panel.
  2. Hover over any icon you want to edit.
  3. Click on Icons button that appear next to the icon.
  4. Choose one of the available icons.
  5. Click on Apply button.

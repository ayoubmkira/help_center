Edit video settings

2 min read

Use the video utility to easily replace or update videos within your sections. This allows you to either select from existing videos or upload new ones.

Replace a Video:

  1. Click on Text Manager or Theme Settings in the left bar.
  2. Hover Over the Desired Video.
  3. Click on the Video Settings Button.
  4. Choose a video or Upload a new one.
  5. You can change the Opacity of the video.
  6. Click on Apply button.

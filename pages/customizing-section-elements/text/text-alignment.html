Text alignment

1 min read

Aligning your text is crucial for enhancing readability and maintaining a clean layout. With the Theme Settings, you can easily adjust the alignment of any text element on your website. Whether you need left, center, or right alignment, this feature ensures that your content looks well-organized across different sections of your site. Follow the steps below to change the alignment of your text.

Text alignment

  1. Click on Theme Settings from the left panel.
  2. Hover over the text element (paragraph, header, etc.) that you want to align.
  3. Click the More Options button that appears next to the text.
  4. Click one of the alignment buttons to set the text to: Left, Center or Right.

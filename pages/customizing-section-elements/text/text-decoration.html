Text decoration

1 min read

Text decoration allows you to add extra emphasis to your text by applying underlines or linethroughs. This is a great way to highlight important words, create links, or visually show edits or removals. Follow the steps below to decorate your text with underlines or linethroughs.

Text decoration

  1. Click on Theme Settings from the left panel.
  2. Hover over the text element (paragraph, header, etc.) that you want to align.
  3. Click the More Options button that appears next to the text.
  4. Apply Text decoration
    • Underline: Click the underline button to add an underline to the selected text.
    • Linethrough: Click the linethrough button to add a line through the text.

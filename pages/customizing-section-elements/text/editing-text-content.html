Editing text content

1 min read

You can easily edit the text content of any section. This allows you to update headings, paragraphs, buttons and other text elements. Follow the steps below to modify your text quickly and efficiently.

Edit text content

  1. Click on Text Manager in the left bar.
  2. Find the text you want to edit.
  3. Click on the text to activate editing mode.
  4. Type your new content.

Change theme colors

2 min read

The Change Theme Colors feature allows you to personalize the color scheme of your website. By modifying the colors for elements such as backgrounds, text, headings, links, and icons, you can create a cohesive and visually appealing design that aligns with your brand or style. Whether you're adjusting colors for light or dark mode, this tool gives you full control over the overall aesthetic of your website.

Change theme colors

  1. Click on Theme Settings from the left panel.
  2. A panel will appear with a list of all available color options, including:
    • Background
    • Font Color
    • Headings (h1, h2, h3, h4)
    • Links
    • Icons
    • And more.
  3. Choose the color for each element in both Light and Dark modes.
  4. Click on Apply button to save the changes.

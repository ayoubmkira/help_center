Edit heading type

1 min read

Headings help structure your content, making it more readable and organized. They define the hierarchy of information on your page and ensure consistency in style based on your Theme Settings.

Change heading type:

  1. Click on Theme Settings in the left panel.
  2. Hover over the desired heading.
  3. A list of buttons (H1 to H4) will appear.
  4. Click on the desired heading type, and it will change automatically.

Tip:

The style of the new heading will automatically follow the styles set in Theme Settings, ensuring consistency across your design.

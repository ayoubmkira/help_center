Replacing videos

2 min read

Use the video utility to easily replace or update videos in your sections. You can choose to embed a new video from a URL or upload a video file, enhancing your content seamlessly.

Replace a video:

  1. Click on Text Manager or Theme Settings in the left bar
  2. Hover Over the Desired Video
  3. Click on the Video Settings Button
  4. Click on Discover Videos to choose a video, or Upload Video to upload a new one
  5. Click on Apply button

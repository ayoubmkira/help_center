Handle forms

1 min read

Forms allow you to collect and manage data on your website. With the form builder, you can easily add, remove, and customize form elements to fit your needs, ensuring a seamless experience for your users.

Handle form:

  1. Choose Theme Settings or Text Manager from the left panel.
  2. Hover over any form element you wish to edit.
  3. Click the Constructor Form button.
  4. A popup will appear with a list of available form elements.
  5. You can drag and drop the elements you want to include in your form.
  6. Inside each element, you can configure its attributes (e.g., placeholder, name, required).

Handle counters & timers

1 min read

Counters and timers are great for creating urgency or displaying important deadlines. You can easily add a countdown to your website and configure what happens once the timer reaches its end.

Handle counters and timers:

  1. Choose Text Manager or Theme Settings from the left panel.
  2. Hover over the timer or counter element you want to edit and click on the Count Down icon.
  3. Enter the deadline date in the provided input field.
  4. Choose the desired action once the countdown ends (None, Modal-Popup, Redirect).
  5. Click Apply button to save the changes.

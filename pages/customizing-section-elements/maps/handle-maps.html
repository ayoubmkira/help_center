Handle maps

1 min read

Maps provide an interactive way to display locations and enhance user experience. By using the map settings, you can easily embed maps from popular services, such as Google Maps, directly onto your website, making it easier for visitors to find and explore relevant locations.

Handle maps

  1. Choose Theme Settings or Text Manager from the left panel.
  2. Hover over the desired map element and click on GMap Settings.
  3. Select your preferred map service (e.g., maps.ie, google.com/maps).
  4. After generating the map iframe, copy it and paste it into the provided field.
  5. Click Apply to add the map to your page.

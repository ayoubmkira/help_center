Adding navigation links

2 min read

To organize and update the navigation links on your website, the builder offers simple tools for adding, editing, and customizing the links. Here's how you can manage your navigation sections effectively.

Steps to Add a New Navigation Link:

  1. Open the Pages Section
    • In the left bar, click on Sections Manager.
    • Hover over the navigation section, then click on Navigation Manager.
    Alternatively:
    • Go to Theme Settings or Text Manager, hover over any link in the navigation bar, and click Change Nav Link.
  2. Add a New Link
    • In the popup that appears, click the Add to Menu button.
    • An accordion will open, displaying all available link options for your navigation menu.
  3. Select Link Type
    • Youâ€™ll see options like:
      • Pages: Choose from existing pages on your site.
      • Product Categories: Add categories of your products.
      • Custom Links: Enter any custom URL you'd like to link to.
      • Blog Posts: Link to specific blog posts.
    • Expand the accordion to select the link type you want to add.
  4. Choose the Link
    • After selecting the type (e.g., Pages), check the box next to the item you want to add to the navigation.
  5. Add the link
    • After selecting your link, click Add to Menu.
    • The link will appear in the list of navigation links.
  6. Reorder Links (Optional)
    • You can reorder the links in your navigation menu by dragging and dropping them. Simply click and hold the link, drag it to a new position, and release.
  7. Apply the changes
    • Once you're satisfied with the links in your menu, Click Apply to save all modifications and update the navigation menu.

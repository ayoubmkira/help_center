Change Logo Size

1 min read

Customizing the size of your website's logo is an essential part of fine-tuning your site's appearance. By adjusting the logo size, you can ensure it fits seamlessly within the navigation bar or footer, offering a balanced and professional layout. Using a simple slider, you have complete control over the size, making it easy to adapt to your design preferences.

Steps to change the logo size:

  1. Click on Text Manager or Theme Settings in the left bar.
  2. Hover over the logo in either the navigation section or footer to reveal options.
  3. Click on the Change Logo Size button.
  4. Use the slider range to adjust the logo size according to your preference.
  5. Click Apply to save the changes.

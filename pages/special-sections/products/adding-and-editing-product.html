Adding products

7 min read

Follow these steps to seamlessly add and configure products in your store. From entering product details and setting variants to uploading images and configuring pricing, discounts, and stock, this guide covers everything needed to successfully list a product. You'll also find options for customizing shipping, tax rules, and customer uploads, ensuring a fully optimized shopping experience.

Step 1: Access the Product Manager

  1. Click on Sections Manager in the left panel.
  2. Click on Products and drag and drop the desired section onto the website.
  3. Hover over the newly added section and click on the Manage Products button.
  4. In the popup that appears, click on Manage Products to access the product list.
  5. Click on Add New Product in the top-right corner to begin adding a new product.

Step 2: Enter Product Information

  1. Name – Enter the product name.
  2. Categories – Select existing categories or create a new one.
  3. Brand – Choose a brand or create a new one:
    • Quickly add by clicking on Add.
    • Create a detailed brand profile by clicking on the link that appears when hovering over the ? icon.
  4. Collection – Select one or multiple collections or create a new one:
    • Quickly add by clicking on Add.
    • Create a detailed collection by clicking on the link that appears when hovering over the ? icon.
  5. Tags – Choose from existing tags or create a new one:
    • Quickly add by clicking on Add.
    • Create detailed tags by clicking on the link that appears when hovering over the ? icon.

Step 3: Configure Product Variants

  1. Product Colors – Select colors or create a new one:
    • Quickly add by clicking on Add and specifying the hex code.
    • Create detailed color options by clicking on the link that appears when hovering over the ? icon.
  2. Choice Options – Select existing options or create a new one:
    • Quickly add by clicking on Add.
    • Create detailed choice options by clicking on the link that appears when hovering over the ? icon.
  3. Add Option Values – Each option will have an input field where you can enter values (e.g., sizes: Small, Medium, Large).

Step 4: Set Product Price & Stock

  • Enter the Price, Cost per Item, and Available Stock for each variant in the provided table.

Step 5: Upload Color Variation Images (if applicable)

  • If the product has color variations, you can upload an image for each color option.

Step 6: Apply Discounts

  1. Enable the Discount option to display a promotional price alongside the original price.
  2. Specify the discount value and choose whether it is a Flat amount or a Percentage.

Step 7: Configure Inventory

  1. Low Stock Quantity – Set a threshold to receive alerts when stock is low.
  2. Minimum Quantity – Define the minimum quantity a buyer must purchase.
  3. Maximum Quantity – Specify the maximum quantity a buyer can buy in one transaction.

Step 8: Add Product Images

  1. Thumbnail Image – Upload a main product image.
  2. Gallery Images – Upload additional images to showcase the product from different angles.

Step 9: Provide a Product Description

  • Add a detailed description, including features, benefits, and specifications.

Step 10: Optimize for Search Engines (SEO)

  1. Meta Title – Enter a title for search engine listings.
  2. Meta Description – Provide a short description for search engines.
  3. Meta Image – Upload an image to be displayed in search engine results.

Step 11: Set Product Status

  1. Published – Check or uncheck to make the product live.
  2. Featured – Check to highlight the product in featured sections.

Step 12: Additional Product Settings

  1. Refundable – Enable or disable the refund option.
  2. Shipping Configuration – Check the given link to configure shipping settings for the product.
  3. Cash on Delivery – Check the given link to enable or disable the cash on delivery option via the Payments Settings.
  4. Shipping Information – Enter details such:
    • Weight, Height, Length, and Width.
  5. Tax Configuration – Set tax rules for the product.
    • Tax Status: Set the tax status for the product.
    • Tax Profile: Choose the applicable tax profile.

    Check the given link to create a new tax profile or manage existing ones.

  6. Customer Uploads – Allow customers to upload documents if enabled.
  7. Product Video – Upload a video showcasing the product, which will be displayed in the product gallery.

Step 13: Finalizing the Product

  1. Click on Update & Draft to save the product as a draft.
  2. Click on Update & Publish to make the product live.
  3. Click the Apply button to confirm all changes.

Quick edit product

3 min read

With the Product Manager, you can easily add, edit, and manage your products. Whether you're setting discounts, updating prices, or managing stock levels, the intuitive interface allows you to make quick adjustments directly from the product list. You can also control product visibility, mark items as featured, and perform actions like editing, deleting, or previewing products, all without leaving the page. This streamlined process ensures that your product catalog stays up-to-date with minimal effort.

Step 1: Access the List of Products

  1. Click on Sections Manager in the left panel.
  2. Click on Products and drag and drop the desired section onto the website.
  3. Hover over the newly added section and click on the Manage Products button.
  4. In the popup that appears, click on Manage Products to access the product list.

Step 2: Quick Edit Product

Once you’ve accessed the product list, you can quickly manage each product using the following options:

  1. Set Discount:
    • Quickly set or update the product's discount value directly from the product list.
  2. Update Price:
    • Adjust the product price without going into the product’s detailed page.
  3. Update Stock:
    • Modify the available stock for the product right from the list.
  4. Control Product Status:
    • Featured: Mark or unmark the product as featured.
    • Published: Toggle the product’s visibility by marking it as published or unpublished.
  5. Other Actions:
    • Edit: Click to edit the product’s detailed information.
    • Delete: Remove the product from the list.
    • Preview: View how the product appears on the website.

These steps provide quick access to essential product management functions without the need to navigate to each individual product page.

Change Logo

1 min read

Your websiteâ€™s branding is essential, and updating your logo ensures consistency across different sections. In the navigation bar and footer, you can easily replace the logo with your preferred design. Additionally, you can modify the favicon, login page background image, and other logo variations (such as dark mode, sticky header, and admin panel logos). This customization helps maintain a professional and cohesive look throughout your website.

Steps to change the logo:

  1. Click on Theme Settings or Text Manager in the left bar.
  2. Hover over the logo in the navigation section or footer to reveal options.
  3. Click on the Change Logo button.
  4. A popup will appear, allowing you to update:
    • Main Logo
    • Favicon
    • Login Page Background Image
    • Other logo variations (Dark, Sticky, Admin, etc.)
  5. Click Apply to save the changes.

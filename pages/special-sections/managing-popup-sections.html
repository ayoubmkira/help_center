Manging Popup Sections

1 min read

Popups are a special type of section that function differently from regular website sections. Instead of appearing directly on the page, they are managed through a dedicated popup list. This allows for greater flexibility in handling modals without affecting the overall layout.

Adding a Popup

  1. Click or hover over Sections Manager in the left sidebar.
  2. Click on Modal Popups to view available popup sections.
  3. Drag and drop a popup section into the website.

Accessing and Editing Popups

  • Unlike normal sections, popups do not remain visible on the page. Instead, an icon in the top right corner of the builder will display all added popups.
  • To access a specific popup:
    1. Click on the popup icon in the top right.
    2. Hover over the desired popup in the list.
    3. Click on the eye icon to make it appear in the builder for editing.

This method allows you to manage popups separately while keeping them accessible when needed.

Navigation Section Limitations

1 min read

The navigation section is a special part of your website and has some restrictions compared to other sections. Here’s what you cannot do with the navigation section:

  • Remove, Duplicate, or Move it.
  • Control Visibility, Alignment, or Padding settings.
  • Modify its JavaScript code.
  • Set an Image or Video as a Background.
  • Changing its content using Smart Creator (AI).

Since the navigation is a core element of your website’s structure, it remains fixed with limited customization options.

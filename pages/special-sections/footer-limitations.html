Footer Section Limitations

1 min read

When working with the footer section of your website, please be aware of the following limitations:

  1. Remove, Duplicate, or Move: The footer cannot be removed, duplicated, or moved. It remains a fixed element within your website's layout to maintain consistency and functionality.
  2. Smart Creator (AI): The Smart Creator (AI) tool cannot be used to modify the content of the footer. Any changes to the footer must be done manually.

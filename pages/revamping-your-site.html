Revamping Your Site

6 min read

Keep your site fresh and engaging by updating its design, content, and features. There are multiple ways to refresh your website, allowing you to choose the approach that best fits your business or brand.

Did you know?

Updating your site signals to search engines that it's active and well-maintained, which can help improve your SEO.

Revamping an Existing Site

Give your online presence a new look by updating your existing site. Depending on your time and resources, you can choose the best method for your needs:

  • On-the-Go Updates: Keep your site live and update each page gradually. This allows you to make changes while keeping your business accessible.
  • Landing Pages: Use a temporary landing page to inform visitors that your site is under maintenance. You can hide the site menu to prevent navigation while working on updates.
  • Unpublishing Your Site: Take your site offline while making changes, then republish when ready.

Duplicating Your Site

Create a duplicate of your existing site to experiment with design changes. This lets you test new features and layouts without affecting your live site. Devaito allows you to create multiple sites, giving you the flexibility to explore different styles.

Important :

Not all content is copied when duplicating a site. Items that do not transfer include:

  • Store orders and invoices
  • Contacts and email campaigns
  • Settings for third-party apps
  • Devaito Code
    • Secrets stored in your Secrets Manager
    • Hidden elements (which require enabling Devaito Code on the new site).

We recommend reviewing your duplicated site to ensure all critical sections and elements are intact.

Restoring a Previous Version

Roll back unwanted changes by restoring a previous version of your site in just a few clicks. This allows you to revert your site's design and content without manually editing everything.

Good to know :

Some components may not be restored when rolling back your site.

Creating a New Site

With Devaito, you have endless possibilities for design and content. If you'd like a completely fresh start, you can create a brand-new site and reassign your existing domain for a whole new experience.

Important :

Your site must be on a premium plan before assigning a domain. If needed, upgrade or reassign an existing plan before proceeding.

Keep in mind :

  • You can have multiple sites under your account.
  • Content is not automatically transferred to new sites.
  • Site-specific features like apps, SEO settings, and marketing integrations do not carry over.

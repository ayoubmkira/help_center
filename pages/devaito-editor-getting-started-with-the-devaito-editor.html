Devaito Editor: Getting Started with the Devaito Editor

5 min read

The **Devaito Editor** is packed with powerful features to help you create a **custom, professional website**. You can **add elements, customize themes, and design every detail** to match your vision.

Getting Started

To begin, go to your site's dashboard and click **Site Customization** then click on Edit My Site to access the Devaito Editor.

Adding & Customizing Elements

The Devaito Editor offers a wide range of customizable sections, including images, text, buttons, and other interactive elements, allowing you to effortlessly design and structure your website to match your vision.

  1. Click on "AI Sections Manager" – Located in the top-left corner of the editor.
  2. Choose Your Section Type – Browse through the available section types.
  3. Drag and Drop the Section – Simply drag your desired section into the workspace.

Once you've added elements, you can customize them to suit your site's look and feel. Click on "Theme Settings" to enable customization. When you hover over an element, its settings will appear, allowing you to adjust it. Depending on the element, different customization options will be available.

Customizing Your Site’s Design

The Devaito Builder offers a user-friendly drag-and-drop interface to easily design your website.

You can customize every aspect of your site:

  • AI Sections Manager : lets you add various section types
  • Theme Settings : allow you to tweak the design elements
  • Text Manager : lets you edit text content
  • Pages Manager : you can easily manage and organize your site's pages

This seamless workflow gives you complete control over your website’s design and content.

Optimizing for Mobile

Since many users browse on mobile, ensure your site looks great on smaller screens.

Use the Mobile Editor to:

  • Adjust element sizes for mobile-friendliness.
  • Ensure text is readable.
  • Hide unnecessary elements for a streamlined mobile experience.

We recommend optimizing your mobile layout **after designing your desktop site**.

Saving & Publishing Your Site

Save your progress regularly and preview changes before going live.

Save: Click Save often to avoid losing work. (Saved changes won’t appear live until published.) Preview: Click Preview to see how your site looks before publishing. Publish: Click Publish when you’re ready to launch your site.

Tip :

Need to restore an older version? Use Site History to revert to previous saves anytime.

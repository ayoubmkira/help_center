Changing a section

2 min read

Access other sections within the same genre to explore different layout options.

Change a Section

  1. Click on AI Sections Manager icon
  2. Hover over the section you want to change.
  3. Browse the available sections in the same genre.
  4. Drag and drop your desired section to replace the current one.

Tip :

The existing section remains on the page, allowing you to experiment with different options.

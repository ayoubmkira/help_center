Adding a Section

2 min read

Adding sections to your website is an essential feature that allows you to structure your content efficiently. Sections help you organize different parts of your webpage, such as headers, footers, product showcases, testimonials, and more. By adding new sections, you can customize your site layout, enhance user experience, and maintain a professional design.

Add a new section :

  1. Click on AI Sections Manager icon
  2. Choose a sections genre
  3. Drag and drop your desired section where you want it.

Your new section is now added, and you can customize it further to match your website's design and content needs.

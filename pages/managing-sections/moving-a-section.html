Moving a section

2 min read

Rearrange sections on your page to maintain a structured and organized layout.

Move a Section

  1. Click AI Sections Manager on the left panel.
  2. Hover over the section you want to move.
  3. Use the arrows to adjust its position:
    • Move Down : Places the section below the one beneath it.
    • Move Up : Moves the section above the one on top.

Save and preview page

1 min read

Once you've made changes to your page, it's important to save your work and preview how it looks before publishing. The website builder provides quick and easy options for both actions.

Saving you page

To ensure your changes are not lost:

  • Click the Save button located at the top right.
  • Your progress will be stored, allowing you to continue editing later.

Previewing Your Page

To see how your page will appear to visitors:

  • Click the Preview button at the top right.
  • A new window will open, displaying your website as it would look when published.

Tip:

The preview will always open the home page by default. If you want to preview a specific page, manually enter its URL in the address bar: (https://yourdomain.com/page/page-name)

Regenerate page content (AI)

2 min read

The AI Page Builder allows you to regenerate an entire page’s content while keeping the navigation and footer unchanged. This feature helps streamline the page creation process by generating relevant content based on your website's needs.

How to Use AI Page Builder

  1. Open AI Page Builder
    • Locate the AI Page Builder button in the middle of the left sidebar and click on it.
  2. Customize Your AI Prompt
    • A popup will appear containing two links that allow you to customize the generated content:
      • Business Activity – Adjusts the AI prompt to focus on business-related content.
      • Ecom Products – Optimizes the AI prompt for e-commerce product pages.
  3. Regenerate the Page
    • Click the Refresh All the Page button to apply AI-generated content to the entire page.

Tip:

The navigation and footer sections will remain unchanged.

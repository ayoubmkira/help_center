Remove page

1 min read

If you no longer need a page, you can easily remove it from your website. Deleting a page helps keep your site organized and prevents unnecessary content from being published.

How to remove a page

  1. Open the Pages Manager from the left panel.
  2. Locate the page you want to delete.
  3. Click the Remove button next to the page name.
  4. Confirm the action when prompted.

Tip:

Once a page is removed, it cannot be restored, so make sure to back up any important content before deleting it.

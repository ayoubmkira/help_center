Preview Page on Different Devices

1 min read

Ensuring that your website looks great on all screen sizes is essential for a seamless user experience. The Builder provides a preview feature that allows you to switch between Desktop, Tablet, and Phone views instantly.

How to Preview Your Page on Different Devices

  1. Locate the Device Preview Icons
    • On the left sidebar, find the device preview icons at the bottom.
  2. Switch Between Views
    • Click on any of the available icons to preview your page in different screen sizes:
      • Desktop View – See how your page appears on larger screens.
      • Tablet View – Check the layout for medium-sized devices.
      • Phone View – Ensure mobile responsiveness for smaller screens.

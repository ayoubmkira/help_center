General settings

1 min read

The general settings of a page allow you to customize its name, title, and visibility. These options help manage how the page appears in navigation and search engines.

Accessing and Editing General Settings

  1. Open the Pages Manager from the left panel.
  2. Locate the page you want to edit.
  3. Click the Page Settings button.
  4. A settings popup will appear, where you can update:
    • Page Name: The internal name used in the builder.
    • Page Title: The title displayed in the browser tab.
    • Visibility: Choose whether the page is shown in the navigation menu.
  5. Click Apply to save your changes.

By adjusting these settings, you can control how each page is displayed and accessed within your website.

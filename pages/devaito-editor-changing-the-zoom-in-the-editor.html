Devaito Editor: Changing the Zoom in the Editor

1 min read

The Devaito Editor does not include a built-in zoom feature, but you can use your browser’s zoom function instead.

  • Press Ctrl + + (Cmd + + on Mac) to zoom in.
  • Press Ctrl + - (Cmd + - on Mac) to zoom out.

Tip :

Pressing Ctrl + 0 (Cmd + 0 on Mac) resets your browser’s zoom to the default setting.

For the best experience and full access to the editor’s tools, it’s recommended to keep your browser’s zoom at 100%.

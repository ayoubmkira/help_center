How to Upgrade Your Site

Upgrade your site anytime to enjoy premium features and exclusive benefits.

To upgrade :

  1. Go to the Upgrade page.
  2. Click Select under your preferred plan.
  3. Choose a billing cycle and click Continue to Checkout.
  5. Enter your payment details and click Submit Purchase.

Note :

After completing your purchase, your bank may require identity verification. If you encounter any issues, contact your bank for assistance.

Creating a New Site from Your Mobile Device

4 min read

Build your Devaito website anytime, anywhere—directly from your mobile device. You can create a site using the Devaito app or by visiting Devaito.com on your browser.

Creating a New Site from the Devaito App

Easily set up a new site on the go with the Devaito app. Your customers can browse your products, read blog posts, schedule services, and stay updated—all from their mobile devices.

Before You Begin :

Download the Devaito app to your mobile device.

To Create a New Site from the Devaito App:

  1. Open the Devaito app on your mobile device.
  2. Choose an option:
    • First-time user? Tap Create Now.
    • Already have a site?
      • Tap your site name at the top
      • Tap + Create New
  3. Choose how to start building your site:
    • Chat with the AI assistant for automated setup.
    • Set up manually for full customization.

What’s Next?

Edit your site anytime, from anywhere—directly from the Devaito app, a mobile browser, or your desktop.

Creating a New Site from Your Mobile Browser

Prefer not to download the app? You can create a new site directly from your mobile browser in just a few steps.

To Create a New Site from Your Mobile Browser:

Prefer not to download the app? You can create a new site directly from your mobile browser in just a few steps.

  1. Log in to Devaito.com from a supported mobile browser.
    New user? Sign up for a free account.
  2. Tap + Create New Site at the bottom.
  3. (First-time users only) Select who the site is for.
    Tip: If you select Client, Freelancer, or Agency, you’ll be prompted to download the Devaito Studio app.
  4. Choose how you want to create your site:
    • AI-assisted setup for a fast, guided experience.
    • Manual setup for full control over your design and features.

Next steps :

Once you have downloaded the Devaito app, edit your site from your mobile device.

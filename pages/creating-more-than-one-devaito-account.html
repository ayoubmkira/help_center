Creating More than One Devaito Account

1 min read

You are welcome to create more than one account with Devaito, completely free of charge. Each account must use a unique email address and username.

How to Create Another Account

Before you begin, ensure you're signed out of your first Devaito account.

To create a new account:

  1. Go to the Devaito sign-up page.
  2. Enter a new email address for your account (make sure itâ€™s different from the one used for your first account).
  3. Re-enter the email address to confirm.
  4. Create a strong password for your new account.
  5. Re-enter the password to confirm.
  6. Click Sign Up to complete the process.

Tip:

You can also sign up quickly using your Facebook or Google account by clicking the corresponding button.

7 Essential Devaito Tools to Use With Your Business Site

5 min read

With hundreds of fully customizable, designer-made business templates, Devaito makes it easy for businesses worldwide to create stunning websites for free.

Whether you need a portfolio site, an online store, or a hotel booking system, Devaito has the right tools to bring your vision to life.

If you haven’t created your site yet, check out our top things to consider before selecting a template or start with a blank one. Devaito also provides powerful features to give you full control over your online presence from start to finish.

Custom Domain

Every Devaito site includes a free default URL in the following format: siteprefix.devaito.com/siteaddress

This lets you go live instantly. However, using a custom domain is essential for building a professional brand. Your domain name is the first impression visitors get, so choose wisely!

When you upgrade to a premium plan, you can connect a custom domain—either one purchased directly from Devaito or one from a third-party provider. Some premium plans also include a free domain voucher for your site.

Business Email with Google Workspace

Once you've upgraded and connected a custom domain, take your branding to the next level with a business email (e.g., info@yourwebsite.com).

A business email helps build credibility and ensures customers reach the right department. You can set up addresses like support@yourbusiness.com or sales@yourbusiness.com to streamline communication.

Learn more about purchasing a Google Workspace business email directly from Devaito.

The Devaito App Marketplace

Extend your site’s capabilities with apps! The Devaito App Marketplace features tools built by Devaito and third-party providers like Etsy, Constant Contact, and Eventbrite.

Not sure where to start? Explore the App Marketplace from your site’s editor, dashboard, or mobile app.

Devaito Payments

Set up Devaito Payments to accept payments seamlessly on your site. This all-in-one system allows you to manage transactions, refunds, and chargebacks from a unified dashboard, with 24/7 dedicated support.

If Devaito Payments isn't available in your region, choose from 70+ alternative payment providers.
